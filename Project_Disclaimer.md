### Project Disclaimer

**Purpose & Scope**
Project Clean Slate is an independent professional portfolio piece authored by Daniel Rodriguez III. It is designed exclusively to demonstrate the technical competencies, data engineering workflows, and analytical problem-solving methodologies relevant to enterprise student information systems and the Workday Student Analyst role.

**Research & Data Rigor**
The operational scenarios, architectural workflows, and organizational mechanics detailed within this project were synthesized through rigorous independent research utilizing strictly publicly available information and industry-standard higher education models. All datasets, event logs, and student profiles are entirely synthetic and programmatically generated. Absolutely no proprietary, confidential, or protected educational data (FERPA) was accessed, utilized, or exposed in the creation of this project.

It does not represent, nor is it endorsed by, partnered with, or affiliated with Texas State Technical College (TSTC) in any capacity. The dashboards, workflows, and strategic recommendations presented herein are independent assessments and should not be construed as official business practices, internal operational records, or the explicit views of TSTC.
